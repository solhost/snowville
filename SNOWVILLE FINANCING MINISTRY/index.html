SNOWVILLE FINANCING MINISTRY

                                                       Snowville relocation plan

___________________________________________________________________________________

Article 1: The issue.

Temperatures are rising, recently even being 2° celsius.

Meaning that the snow is starting to melt. And it seems that it will be completely melted with-in a month, And near the end of march there will be nothing left of Snowville. The snowlevens, Government office, jail and housing will be gone by the start of spring. We need a way to rebuild snowville with other materials when the snow melts.

Article 2: The Solution (sort of)

Either snowville will disband until snow starts falling again, or we rebuild housing and buildings with literal sticks and stones. Or build a weather machine.

Article 3: General stuff.

We only have power over the snow that’s already there, not the weather. So we are cooked until next winter.
